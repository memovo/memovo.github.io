Game Overview

Learn Indian Scripts is an interactive game designed to help you master reading Indian scripts through practice and repetition. The game adapts to your skill level and provides immediate feedback to help you improve.

Pro Tip: Regular practice is key to mastering any script. Try to play for at least 10-15 minutes daily for the best results.

Game Levels

Level 1: Basics

Start your journey with fundamental characters

Target: 0.50 seconds per letter
  • Independent vowels
  • Basic consonants
  • Simple recognition
  • 20 letters per level

Level 2: Matras

Master vowel diacritics and combinations

Target: 0.50 seconds per letter
  • Vowel diacritics
  • Consonant-vowel combinations
  • Complex recognition
  • 20 letters per level

Level 3: Advanced

Challenge yourself with complex combinations

Free play mode
  • Conjunct consonants
  • Complex combinations
  • Advanced recognition
  • Unlimited practice

Game Controls

Multiple Choice

Click on the correct transliteration for each character

Voice Input

Speak the transliteration using your microphone

Pause/Resume

Press Space or click the Pause button to take a break

Reset Progress

Start over with the Reset button when needed

Progress Tracking

The game tracks your performance through several metrics:

  • Average time per letter
  • Correct and incorrect answers
  • Level progression
  • Overall accuracy
Remember: Focus on accuracy first, then speed. It's better to take a little longer and get it right than to rush and make mistakes.
